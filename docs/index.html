An Inventory Overview
Downtown, there are
26,267 parking spaces
  • 8,311 in surface lots
  • 13,904 in structured garages
  • 1,832 underground
  • 2,220 on-street
They take up
4,068,655 square feet
☞ That’s 18.725% of the Downtown land area!
And of all spaces, only
303 are handicap accessible
